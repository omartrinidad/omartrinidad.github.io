Loneliness and love

During a lot of time, loneliness was not a problem, I truly enjoyed to be alone in the highschool, I read a lot during that period.

I have been alone most of time during these thirty years, at the middle of my twenties, this became a horrible thing.

Happily, I still have few friends that helped me in hard moments, so, I am not totally alone.

Being the good and turning the other cheek does not work, at least not for me, at least not in every case. Why is so hard to find real friends?

I am feeling empty. For me is too complicate (it is impossible) to forgive.

I have been in love with Irasu and Miriam during the highschool, and Valeria during the highschool.

The thing with Irasu never worked, she hit me. With Miriam and Valeria was different, I was not able to give the last step. Yes, I am a mother fucker.

I don't give a shit to write the name of the girls, very likely they won't read this.

And then, happened again, I fall in love during last year. I even learned to ride a bike because of that, and that is interesting, because I never tried to ride a bike after I fell down so badly when I was a kid. Once again, as with Irasu, I was hitted. Even worse, I was swinged. As one song in Spanish says: Ácabame de matar, pa' qué me dejas herido.

I became thirty on January, I was alone in my room, I woke up late, when it was already dark, it was too cold. It was horrible and it has been horrible since then. I was alone, it was one week after the New Year eve, when I was also alone, and I ate a ramen soup, it was one week after Christmas, where I was also alone.

I have lost the motivation. I really don't want to live anymore.
